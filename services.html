Our Programmes

Explore how we're building a reading culture across South Africa

Literacy for All (Children's Programme)

Wednesdays & Fridays | 3PM - 5PM

Weekly after-school reading clubs held at our Braamfontein and Soweto hubs. Children aged 6-12 engage with age-appropriate books, participate in storytelling sessions, and receive take-home books to build home libraries.

150+ children currently enrolled
Free snacks and learning materials
Take-home books for every child

Adult Literacy & Numeracy

Mondays & Thursdays | 6PM - 8PM

Evening classes for adults who never had the opportunity to learn to read. Our patient, trained volunteers work one-on-one or in small groups to build foundational literacy skills, from basic letter recognition to reading comprehension and basic maths.

Flexible, self-paced learning
One-on-one tutoring available
Certificate of achievement

Mobile Library Service

Rotating schedule - 8 locations weekly

Our converted minibus brings books to remote areas and schools without library access. The mobile library carries over 2,000 books and visits 8 locations weekly, providing free book borrowing and storytelling sessions.

Free book borrowing for all ages
Storytelling sessions during stops
Book donation collection points
